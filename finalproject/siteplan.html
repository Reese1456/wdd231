Site Plan for JRLT E‑Commerce

Site Name

JRLT E‑Commerce

This site name was selected to reflect a modern and dynamic online store that offers a curated selection of apparel (T‑Shirts and Hoodies) with a focus on quality and style. The name also ties in with the existing branding (JRLT Logo) used throughout the project.

Potential Domain: jrlt-ecommerce.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of JRLT E‑Commerce is to provide a simple, elegant online storefront for showcasing and selling apparel, specifically T‑Shirts and Hoodies. The site aims to offer a seamless shopping experience with visually appealing product presentations, an intuitive navigation structure, and a simulated checkout process to mimic a real e-commerce experience.

Scenarios

Below are a few scenarios that our target audience might have when visiting the site:

  • Scenario 1: "What are the unique design features of the available T‑Shirts, and which one best suits my style?"
  • Scenario 2: "How can I quickly compare hoodie options and easily add my favorite items to the cart?"

Color Schema

The website uses the following color scheme:

  • Primary Dark (var(--primary-dark)): #000000 – Used for header and footer backgrounds and key text on light backgrounds.
  • Light Background (var(--light-bg)): #EDEDED – Applied to the body background and for text on dark elements.
  • Accent (var(--accent)): #3F3F3F – Utilized for headings and accents throughout the site.
  • Card Background (var(--card-bg)): #B6B6B6 – Used as the background for product cards and other information panels.

Typography

The site uses the following fonts:

  • Headings: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, sans-serif – Used for main headings (h1, h2, h3) to provide a clean, modern look.
  • Body Text: Arial, sans-serif – Provides readability and a classic web-safe option for paragraphs and other text content.
